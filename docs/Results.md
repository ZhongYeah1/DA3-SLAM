# Results

## Headline result (KITTI validation set, 13 drives)

DA3-SLAM with `da3nested-giant-large-1.1` beats the VGGT-SLAM baseline on
**ATE 12/13, AbsRel 12/13, RMSE_d 11/13, δ<1.25 13/13**. Biggest wins on
long moving drives: 0023 ATE 36.8 → 2.4 m (15×), 0036 53.2 → 8.4 m (6×),
0047 22.0 → 10.2 m (2×). Side-by-side comparisons are generated by
`evals/compare_vggt_vs_nested.py`.

### KITTI validation (13 drives, `da3-small`, `min_disparity=15`)

Driver: `./evals/eval_kitti.sh` -- runs each `(method, drive)` pair, writes a
TUM trajectory + per-frame depth `.npy`, then scores with `evo_ape -as`
(ATE RMSE) and `evals/eval_kitti_depth.py` (Eigen crop, median align).

Mean over all 13 val drives (156 runs, `da3-small`, `min_disparity=15`
unless swept):

| Method                           | ATE RMSE [m] ↓ | AbsRel ↓   | RMSE [m] ↓ | δ<1.25 ↑   |
|----------------------------------|---------------:|-----------:|-----------:|-----------:|
| `vggt_slam`                      |          11.48 | **0.069**  |       4.33 | **0.938**  |
| **`da3_baseline`**               |       **4.64** |      0.084 |       4.34 |      0.915 |
| `da3_refine` (`bounded_clip`)    |           5.80 |      0.085 |   **4.29** |      0.913 |
| `da3_refine` (`inplace`)         |           7.67 |      0.085 |   **4.29** |      0.913 |
| `da3_refine` (`none`)            |       **4.64** |      0.085 |   **4.29** |      0.913 |
| `da3_large_refine`               |           9.00 |      0.085 |       4.32 |      0.912 |
| `da3_baseline` (`scale=ransac`)  |           4.73 |      0.084 |       4.34 |      0.915 |
| `da3_baseline` (`scale=weighted`)|           7.70 |      0.084 |       4.34 |      0.915 |
| `da3_baseline` (chunk=32 ov=4)   |           5.00 |      0.086 |       4.40 |      0.911 |
| `da3_baseline` (chunk=60 ov=8)   |          16.92 |      0.087 |       4.43 |      0.909 |
| `da3_baseline` (min_disp=10)     |           6.13 |      0.084 |       4.35 |      0.915 |
| `da3_baseline` (min_disp=25)     |           5.13 |      0.086 |       4.34 |      0.910 |

**Takeaways:**

- **`da3_baseline` is Pareto on ATE.** All seven DA3 ablations regress or
  tie on mean ATE; `da3_refine` with `--reopt_method none` is the only
  variant that matches baseline exactly (same poses, pass-2 depth swap
  is ATE-neutral by construction).
- **Graph reopt hurts without helping depth.** `bounded_clip` costs +25%
  ATE, `inplace` +65%; both produce per-frame depth identical to `none`
  (depth is written before reopt runs).
- **Chunk size dominates robustness.** chunk=60 ov=8 blows up long
  no-LC drives (mean ATE 16.9 m) -- per-chunk pose error amplifies over
  the trajectory when submap count shrinks.
- **Depth is nearly invariant across DA3 variants.** AbsRel spans
  0.084–0.087 across all ten DA3 ablations; depth gain on KITTI is
  bounded by DA3's per-frame prediction, not by pose quality.
- **VGGT-SLAM loses on ATE (11.5 m) but wins on depth (AbsRel 0.069).**
  Different failure modes -- DA3 is a more robust pose estimator; VGGT's
  depth is sharper where it doesn't hard-fail.

### KITTI -- big-backbone sweep (2026-04-19)

Same 13 drives, same driver, `min_disparity=15`:

| Method (backbone)                             | ATE RMSE [m] ↓ | AbsRel ↓   | RMSE [m] ↓ | δ<1.25 ↑   |
|-----------------------------------------------|---------------:|-----------:|-----------:|-----------:|
| `da3_baseline` (`da3-giant`, 1.15B)           |          3.918 |     0.0503 |      2.908 |     0.9685 |
| `da3_refine[none]` (`da3-giant`)              |          3.918 |     0.0505 |      2.889 |     0.9690 |
| **`da3_baseline` (`da3nested-giant-large-1.1`, 1.40B)** | **2.431** | **0.0406** |  **2.609** | **0.9780** |
| `da3_refine[none]` (nested)                   |          2.431 |     0.0429 |      2.630 |     0.9769 |
| `da3_refine[inplace]` (nested)                |          2.550 |     0.0429 |      2.631 |     0.9769 |

**Takeaways:**

- **Backbone size dominates ATE.** Going `small → giant → nested` on the
  same pipeline drops mean ATE 4.64 → 3.92 → 2.43 m (−48% vs small),
  outweighing any pass-2 or scale-method ablation by 4×+.
- **Depth improves monotonically with backbone.** AbsRel 0.084 → 0.050
  → 0.041. At nested, DA3-SLAM beats VGGT-SLAM on depth (0.041 vs 0.069)
  *and* on ATE (2.43 vs 11.48 m).
- **Refinement is still net-neutral or slightly negative.** At nested,
  refinement bumps AbsRel from 0.0406 to 0.0429; trajectory-wise `refine[none]`
  ties baseline and `refine[inplace]` regresses +5%.
- **Practical default on KITTI.** `--model_name da3nested-giant-large-1.1 --strategy baseline`.
